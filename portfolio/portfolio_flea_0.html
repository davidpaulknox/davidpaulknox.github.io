F.L.E.A.
The player pilots the hopping F.L.E.A. tank through a serries of levels.  The F.L.E.A. is armed with a limited number of sticky bombs which it can shoot at enemies or attach to surfaces.  In addition to destroying enemy tanks, the F.L.E.A. can jump onto the attached sticky bombs to get a boost jump.  Much of the gameplay revolves around use of these sticky bombs. Later levels use a flare mechanic where the F.L.E.A. must launch flares to light its way through a dark level.
