MONSTER MAFIA is a twin stick shooter created in Unreal Development Kit by a team of 13 over the course of five months.  The game is set to a classic movie monster theme, having players fight iconic monsters such as the Creature from the Black Lagoon, the Invisible Man, and the Wolfman.

• Assessed and critiqued level designs
